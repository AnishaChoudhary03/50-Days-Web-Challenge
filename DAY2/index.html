Empowering the Next Generation of Engineers

Synexus is a student community where beginners learn by building. We run workshops, hackathons, and projects so members can grow their skills together.

See our Initiatives

About Synexus

Synexus brings together students from Information Science, Electronics, and Computer Science backgrounds. Our goal is to help students go from classroom learning to real projects, through workshops, hackathons, and mentorship from the core team. No prior experience is needed to join.

Initiatives

Campus Hack Sprint

A 24 hour hackathon where teams build a working project based on a real campus problem. Judged by student leads and faculty mentors.

Open Source Onboarding Lab

Weekly sessions to help beginners make their first open source contribution, covering Git basics and how to pick a good starter issue.

Speaker & Industry Bridge

Monthly talks with alumni and working engineers about skills that are useful in real jobs but not always taught in class.
