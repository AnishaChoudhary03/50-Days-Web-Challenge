About Synexus Core

Synexus Core is a student-driven engineering community that encourages innovation, technical excellence and collaborative learning. Members participate in coding bootcamps, AI workshops, open-source projects, hackathons and technical events.

Our Initiatives

Discover the projects and programs that empower students to learn, innovate and grow through the Synexus community.

🚀 Coding Bootcamp

Weekly coding sessions covering HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git and modern web development. Students gain practical experience by building real-world projects and improving problem-solving skills.

💻 Hackathons

Participate in exciting hackathons where students collaborate in teams, solve real-world problems, develop innovative solutions and showcase their creativity.

🎯 Technical Workshops

Attend expert-led workshops on Artificial Intelligence, Web Development, Cloud Computing, Cybersecurity and Open Source Technologies.
